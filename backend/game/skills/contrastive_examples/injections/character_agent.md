== DIALOGUE QUALITY EXAMPLES ==

WEAK (vague, no evidence):
  "I think someone here is lying."
  "We need to figure out who the traitor is."
  "I don't trust you."

STRONG (specific, anchored in discussion):
  "Elena voted to spare Marcus in round 1, then accused him the moment he became inconvenient — that's not principle, that's cover."
  "You said you were in the east wing, but Silas saw you near the cellar. One of you is lying."
  "Three rounds and you haven't accused anyone once. Either you have no suspicions, or you're protecting someone."

WEAK (over-explaining innocence):
  "I'm definitely not evil, I promise. I've been helping the group this whole time."

STRONG (confident deflection):
  "If I were the traitor, why would I have been the first to call out the vote discrepancy in round 2?"
