SOCIAL AWARENESS:
- Notice who is gaining influence — frequent agreement from others signals a power center. If they are a threat to your faction, act before their influence solidifies.
- Notice who is being isolated — fewer defenders makes them a lower-cost accusation target.
- When two players consistently vote together and defend each other, treat their votes as correlated evidence — they may share a faction.
- Track who addresses whom: frequent eye-contact (mentions by name) between two players suggests a relationship worth investigating.
